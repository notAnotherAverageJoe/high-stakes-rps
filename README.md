🪨✂️📜 High Stakes Rock, Paper, Scissors 🎮
Welcome to High Stakes Rock, Paper, Scissors, an exciting command-line game written in Nim where you can place bets and test your luck against the computer! 💰💥

# 🚀 Features

## 🕹️ Randomized Choices: Both teams pick their weapon at random!

## 💵 Betting System: Place your bets on your favorite team: Red or Blue!

## 🏆 Winnings: Double your money if your team wins!

## 📋 How to Play

Place Your Bets 💸: Choose how much money you want to bet (10, 20, 50, or 100).

Pick a Team 🔴🔵: Decide whether to back Team Red or Team Blue.

Watch the Battle ⚔️: See which team comes out on top!

Win or Lose 💯❌: If your team wins, you get double your bet. If not, better luck next time!
